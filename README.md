🎯 Vision:

PiC teaches users how to connect to their Personal Information Center for self-healing. 
The body is the ultimate database, containing all the answers regarding any illness, condition, or symptom. 
All the answers already exist within you; the PiC app provides the tools and methodology to connect to this information and use it to lead your own healing process.

🛠️ Core Principles:

Ownership & Responsibility: The system is a hub for learning and action that empowers users to take full responsibility for their health. 
The user manages their healing process based on a deep understanding of their needs and the precise treatment required at any given moment.

Intent & Symptom Bucket: The system maintains the symptom list as a persistent visual anchor. Users can always return to and view the symptoms they are currently addressing.

The Empty Vessel (Brain Dump): The healing process begins with clearing mental space. The "Brain Dump" (clearing the clutter) is a therapeutic step that allows for new content and learning to enter.

Atomic Focus: Information is presented step-by-step (one screen, one action) to prevent cognitive overload and maintain clarity, specifically optimized for ADHD.

State Management (Daily Flow): A dynamic "Action Plan" manages multiple parallel processes: daily treatments, active courses, and new insights.

💎 Features & Ecosystem:

A. The Academy (The Gateway)
Foundational Course: A free introductory course teaching the principles of the method, how to perform muscle testing, and the art of precise inquiry.

B. The Inquiry Engine
Brain Dump: A space to input and rate symptoms (1-10) to establish therapeutic priorities.

Smart Mapping: Automatic linking of inquiry results and treatments to the specific symptom groups defined during the session.

C. Courses & Journeys
Modular Structure: The ability to build developmental sequences that combine written guidance, library treatments, inspirational insights, and self-reflection tasks.

D. The Reflective Journal (The Archive)
Contextual Archive: A chronological journal aggregating all self-documentation (thoughts, post-treatment reflections, insights). Every entry is automatically linked to the context in which it was written.

E. Service & Community
Referral System: If the inquiry process indicates a need for external support, the system suggests referrals to doctors or certified PiC practitioners.

Tribes: Supporting the formation of user communities based on shared topics or under a practitioner’s guidance.

🏗️ Technical Architecture:

Stack: Developed with Flutter and Supabase (PostgreSQL).

Data Model: A modular structure supporting polymorphism (flexible content types) and separation between static knowledge and user activity.

Data Privacy & Control: Users have the absolute right to permanently delete all their personal data from the app's databases at any time, upholding the principle of information ownership.

Collaborative Mode: Readiness for a "Practitioner View" for secure data sharing, contingent upon a strict authentication process.

AI Ready: An organized data infrastructure that allows for future integration of an AI Agent to analyze personal trends within the journal.
